History & Traditional Use

Magnesium has been used medicinally for centuries, but earlier forms like magnesium oxide had limitations. The development of chelated forms in the late 20th century revolutionized supplementation. Glycinate in particular emerged from research on amino acid transporters that facilitate mineral uptake across the intestinal barrier.
