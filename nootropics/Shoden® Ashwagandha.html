Clinical studies on Shoden® Ashwagandha have found effective results at doses as low as 120 mg per day, due to its high bioavailability. For general health, 120–240 mg daily is common. Athletes and those under heavy stress may use up to 360 mg daily, split into morning and evening doses.

Shoden® Ashwagandha is well-tolerated in most individuals. Mild side effects may include digestive discomfort or drowsiness in sensitive users. It should be avoided during pregnancy and by individuals with hyperthyroidism unless under medical supervision.
