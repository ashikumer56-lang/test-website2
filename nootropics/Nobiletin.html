What is Nobiletin?

Nobiletin is a polymethoxylated flavone abundant in citrus peels, particularly in tangerines and oranges. Unlike many flavonoids, it crosses the blood-brain barrier to exert neuroprotective, anti-inflammatory, and circadian-regulating effects.

  • Blood-Brain Barrier Permeable: Delivers direct effects on neural tissues.
  • Anti-Inflammatory: Reduces cytokines and oxidative markers.
  • Circadian Support: Activates BMAL1 and PER2 clock genes.
  • Neuroplasticity: Promotes BDNF, CREB signaling, and synaptogenesis.
