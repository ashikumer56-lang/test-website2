What Is Alpha-GPC?

Alpha-GPC (L-Alpha Glycerylphosphorylcholine) is a natural phospholipid choline source, found in soy and sunflower lecithin and synthesized in trace amounts by your body. Its unique glycerophosphate head allows it to cross the blood–brain barrier efficiently, making it one of the most potent choline supplements on the market.

Unlike cheaper forms such as choline bitartrate or citrate, Alpha-GPC raises brain choline levels quickly, fueling acetylcholine-dependent processes like memory encoding, attention, and muscle contraction. It also supports neuronal membrane integrity, making it a dual-action nootropic and neuroprotectant.

Additionally, Alpha-GPC has been shown to support cognitive resilience under stress and fatigue, making it a go-to ingredient in many nootropic stacks for sustained mental energy.

Side Effects & Safety

Alpha-GPC is generally well-tolerated. Side effects are rare and mild when dosing within recommended ranges:

  • Mild Headache or Brain Fog: Often results from choline overload; titrate down if headache occurs and retitrate when minimal dose discovered.
  • Nausea or Digestive Upset: At high doses (>1,200 mg/day), some experience mild GI discomfort. Taking with food often alleviates these symptoms.
  • Insomnia: Due to increased mental energy; avoid dosing within 4 hours of bedtime. Stick to morning or early afternoon doses.

Tip: Start at 300 mg and slowly increase until optimal cognitive or performance effect is reached. Monitor tolerance weekly.
