What Is Black Ginger?

Botanical Name: Kaempferia parviflora

Common Names: Thai black ginger, Krachai Dam

Active Constituents: Methoxyflavones (5,7-dimethoxyflavone, 5,7,4’-trimethoxyflavone), polyphenols

Black Ginger’s unique dark root contains concentrated flavones that modulate energy pathways, support circulation, and protect neurons from age-related damage. It’s valued in traditional Thai medicine for enhancing stamina and metabolic health.
