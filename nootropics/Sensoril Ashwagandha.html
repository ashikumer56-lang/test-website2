History & why a branded extract?

Ashwagandha has been used within Ayurveda for centuries as a Rasayana — a tonic that supports vitality, longevity and mental resilience. As global interest grew, manufacturers created branded extracts like Sensoril® and KSM‑66® to deliver reproducible effects across batches. Sensoril®’s inclusion of leaf material provides a different phytochemical balance compared to root‑only extracts, and multiple clinical studies have used Sensoril® specifically — making it one of the better researched, standardized Ashwagandha options for therapeutic use.

Safety, side effects & interactions

Sensoril® is generally well tolerated; reported adverse effects in trials are infrequent and mild. Still, observe these cautions:

  • Possible drowsiness at higher doses — adjust timing accordingly.
  • Mild digestive discomfort in sensitive individuals; taking with food usually helps.
  • Potential interaction with sedatives, thyroid medications, and immunosuppressants — consult your clinician if you are on prescription medicines.
  • Pregnancy & breastfeeding: insufficient data — avoid unless directed by a healthcare provider.

Monitoring tip: If on thyroid replacement or other endocrine therapy, check TSH/T3/T4 and clinical symptoms after starting Sensoril®.

Troubleshooting & optimization

  • Too sedated: Reduce dose or move dosing to morning; try half the dose at night if using for sleep.
  • Not feeling benefits: Ensure at least 4–8 weeks of consistent use at a therapeutic dose; pair with lifestyle changes (sleep, exercise).
  • Vivid dreams: Reduce evening dose or take earlier in day.
