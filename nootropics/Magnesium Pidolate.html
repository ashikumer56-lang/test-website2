What Is Magnesium Pidolate?

Magnesium Pidolate is magnesium bound to pidolic acid (5-oxoproline), facilitating enhanced crossing of the blood–brain barrier. Pidolic acid plays dual roles: a glutamate cycle intermediate and a transporter enhancer, guaranteeing efficient magnesium delivery to neurons.

Key attributes:

  • CNS Penetration: Elevated brain magnesium levels compared to other salts.
  • Buffered Absorption: Gradual release minimizes GI upset.
  • Neurological Specificity: Supports glutamate/GABA homeostasis.
  • Clinical Utility: Used in deficiency states, mood disorders, and neuromuscular conditions.
