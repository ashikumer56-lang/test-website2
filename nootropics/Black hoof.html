What Is Black Hoof?

Common Names: Mesimakobu (Japan), Sang-hwang (Korea), Song-Gen (China)

Active Compounds: Beta-glucans, hispolon, ergosterol peroxide

Traditional Uses: Immune tonics, digestive health, anti-inflammatory remedies

Black Hoof grows on mulberry and oak trees, forming hoof-like brackets. Modern extracts concentrate its polysaccharides and flavonoids to harness immune-boosting and neuroprotective effects, supporting both peripheral and central health.
